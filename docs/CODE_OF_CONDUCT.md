## Summary

All contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and inclusive toward all participants
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Avoid harassment, trolling, or discriminatory language
- Protect the privacy of others

Violations may be reported to Tom Burchell at **w0516036@nscc.ca**.

See [../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for the complete policy.
